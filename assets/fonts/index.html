MIDTOWN’S MOST CELEBRATED ADDRESS

A peerless address for business, a glamorous setting for society weddings, and a treasured retreat for family holidays, Lotte New York Palace has long reigned as New York City’s most iconic and instantly recognizable hotel. Offering two remarkable experiences within The Palace and The Towers, this stately and historic landmark exudes sophistication and luxury in Midtown Manhattan.

AT THE CENTER OF IT ALL

Located on Madison Avenue at 50th Street, Lotte New York Palace overlooks St. Patrick's Cathedral and Rockefeller Center. Shopping, theater, museums and dining are just steps away. The hotel’s Villard Mansion is listed on the National Register of Historic Places and is a New York City designated landmark.
